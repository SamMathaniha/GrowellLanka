WELCOME TO GROWELL LANKA (PVT) LTD!

- Your Trusted and leading property management company in Sri Lanka -


MR. JUDE FERNANDO

Every great journey starts with a single step. We took the very first step with sheer hard work and a dedicated team of 7 individuals, in our mind to provide scaled new heights of workplace excellence. Growell Lanka Pvt Ltd (GLPL), the journey began in 2019 with 8 staff in a single site was a humble beginning.

Since our inception in 2019, we have carved a solid business foundation through sheer determination and passion to deliver world-class solutions. Today GLPL is a name to reckon with and is highly respected in the facility management sector. We owe our humble success to our hard-working team and our gracious clients equally.

We believe in the People service profit concept. Our highly experienced facilities management team works with clients to ensure safe, compliant, and professional built environments through evaluation, maintenance, repair, and upgrade of their facilities.

We assign the right people to the right place at the right time to achieve the best outcomes. This improves and standardizes service provision across a portfolio and creates significant cost savings for our clients. we provide a complete facilities management service to clients in both public and private sectors across Srilanka

Powered by integrated management, we deliver Facilities Management solutions that connect and elevate all aspects of your operations. An end-to-end operating model unites people, assets, data, technology, budgets, and contracts to drive best-in-class performance and business productivity. Our solutions support distinct client requirements including multi-national enterprises, local single-market companies, on-demand facility support, data centers, critical operations, and more.
